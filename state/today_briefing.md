# Daily AI Recruitment Briefing — 11 July 2026

## AI in Recruitment — What's New
- **Staffing firms are pivoting away from commodity CV-screening toward scarce, AI-era specialties**, as cheap AI tools erode the value of high-volume sourcing work. Randstad reports demand for AI solutions leads, process automation specialists, and AI architects up 226%, 196%, and 152% respectively, while Robert Walters is deliberately moving away from junior, easily-automated roles to focus on "orchestrating" clients' broader talent strategy. ([Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-07-09/recruiters-shift-focus-to-specialized-ai-jobs-to-stay-relevant)) (9 July)

**Bottom line:** With AI now commoditizing high-volume sourcing and screening, TA leaders should audit where their own team's value still lies in judgment and orchestration rather than volume — before that work gets automated out from under them too.
