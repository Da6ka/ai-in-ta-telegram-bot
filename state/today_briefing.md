# Daily AI Recruitment Briefing — 11 July 2026

## Claude & Anthropic in TA
- **California struck a first-of-its-kind deal giving all state agencies — plus cities and counties — access to Claude at a 50% discount, with free Anthropic-led workforce training and workflow support.** When the largest US state standardizes public-sector workers on Claude and bundles reskilling, it both models an enterprise AI-training playbook and signals which Claude fluency TA teams should start sourcing and upskilling for. ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/29/anthropic-and-gov-newsom-forge-deal-allowing-california-government-to-use-claude-at-half-price/)) (29 June)

## AI in Recruitment — What's New
- **AI skills now appear in 73% of US tech job postings as of May, up from 71% in April and 192% higher year over year, per Dice's latest Tech Jobs Report.** For TA leaders this is the clearest signal yet to move to skills-based sourcing and rewrite reqs around demonstrated AI fluency rather than legacy titles. ([CIO Dive](https://www.ciodive.com/news/dice-report-tech-hiring-AI/824893/)) (10 July)
- **Recruitable added business-development and hiring-intelligence features to its agency recruitment platform**, letting staffing firms track live hiring signals to find prospective clients alongside candidate sourcing in one system. It's a concrete example of AI pushing agencies up-market into demand-generation as commodity sourcing gets automated. ([Barchart](https://www.barchart.com/story/news/3120235/recruitable-adds-hiring-intelligence-to-ai-recruitment-platform)) (1 July)
- **Folks shipped AI-powered candidate matchmaking, resume analysis, and job-description/communication drafting into its ATS.** A useful reminder that mid-market ATS vendors are now embedding generative matching by default — evaluate whether the matching logic is auditable before relying on its rankings. ([GlobeNewswire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/07/02/3321386/0/en/folks-revolutionizes-recruitment-with-ai-powered-updates-to-ats.html)) (2 July)

**Bottom line:** AI fluency is fast becoming table stakes in the roles you're hiring for and the tools you're hiring with — prioritize skills-based sourcing and insist on auditable matching before you scale any vendor's AI features.
