# Daily AI Recruitment Briefing — 1 July 2026

## Claude & Anthropic in TA
- Claude's context-awareness lets recruiters send up to 200 genuinely personalized outreach messages a day versus the traditional ~20, shifting the bottleneck from volume to judgment. ([HeroHunt](https://www.herohunt.ai/blog/recruiting-with-claude-ai-the-complete-2026-guide/))
- Agentic setups via MCP now let Claude connect directly to ATS and interview platforms, acting as an operational copilot that pulls candidate data and hiring analytics in real time rather than just drafting text. ([Metaview](https://www.metaview.ai/resources/blog/claude-for-recruiters))
- Anthropic's new Claude Corps Fellowship places 100 fellows inside nonprofits and public-interest orgs starting October 2026 ($85K + benefits); applications close 17 July — worth flagging to early-career candidates. ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-corps))

## AI in Recruitment — What's New
- AI use across HR tasks jumped to 43% in 2026 (up from 26% in 2024), and 95% of U.S. hiring managers expect to increase AI investment — pilots are now production workflows. ([MSH](https://www.talentmsh.com/insights/ai-in-recruitment))
- Over half of firms using AI screening saw KPIs improve by 25%+; combining AI screening with human-led final interviews cut time-to-hire by 40% while lifting first-year retention by 25%. ([SelectSoftware Reviews](https://www.selectsoftwarereviews.com/blog/ai-recruiting-statistics))
- Only 26% of applicants trust AI to evaluate them fairly, making visible human oversight and clear explanations table stakes — position AI as co-pilot, not autopilot. ([ATSOndemand](https://power.atsondemand.com/top-ai-recruiting-trends-2026-the-rise-of-human-ai-partnership-in-talent-acquisition/))
- Compliance is tightening: EU AI Act obligations for general-purpose AI took effect August 2026, while NYC's Local Law 144 still mandates annual bias audits and candidate notices for automated hiring tools. ([MSH](https://www.talentmsh.com/insights/ai-in-recruitment))
- Skills-based hiring and end-to-end automation (sourcing, scheduling, conversation summaries) are the defining 2026 shifts, freeing recruiters for strategic, relationship-driven work. ([Metaview](https://www.metaview.ai/resources/blog/recruiting-trends))
