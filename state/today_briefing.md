# Daily AI Recruitment Briefing — 15 July 2026

## Claude & Anthropic in TA
- **Anthropic launched Claude for Teachers, giving verified US K-12 educators free premium Claude access, standards-aligned curricula, and an "AI Fluency for K-12 Teachers" course co-built with Teach for America and the AFT.** It isn't a hiring tool, but it's Anthropic seeding Claude fluency into the earliest end of the talent pipeline — worth watching as "AI-fluent" hardens into a baseline candidate expectation. ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-for-teachers)) (15 July 2026)

## AI in Recruitment — What's New
- **Eightfold moved its "Infinite Workforce" capabilities to general availability, including 360 Interviews — which collapses screening, functional, and technical assessment into a single agent-orchestrated interview — and Workforce Readiness, a board-ready dashboard scoring how much of each role AI can do versus what the workforce actually captures.** Agent-run, end-to-end evaluation is now shipping in a major enterprise TA platform; pressure-test the interview agent for auditability and adverse impact before letting it gate candidates. ([Eightfold](https://eightfold.ai/blog/how-to-build-infinite-workforce/)) (15 July 2026)
- **The US Department of Labor awarded nearly $162M in pay-for-performance grants to expand Registered Apprenticeships across AI, semiconductor, nuclear, IT, and telecom sectors, with Jobs for the Future taking $40M earmarked for AI, semiconductor, and nuclear roles.** For TA and workforce leaders, it's a concrete, funded channel to build AI-adjacent talent pipelines rather than competing for scarce lateral hires. ([US Department of Labor](https://www.dol.gov/newsroom/releases/eta/eta20260707)) (7 July 2026)

**Bottom line:** AI is being wired into both ends at once — the talent pipeline (federal apprenticeship money, Anthropic seeding classroom AI fluency) and the hiring stack itself (Eightfold's agent-run interviews) — so invest now in building AI-fluent supply while auditing any agentic evaluation tool before it decides who advances.
