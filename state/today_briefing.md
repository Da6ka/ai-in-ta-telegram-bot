## AI in Recruitment — What's New
- **Two people-ops leaders — from Morning Brew and Equifax — detailed how they're deploying AI agents to offload benefits, onboarding, and other repetitive HR questions**, a concrete look at where agentic AI is actually landing in lean HR teams rather than in hype decks. ([How two HR executives are using AI tools with their people ops teams — HR Brew](https://www.hr-brew.com/stories/how-two-hr-executives-are-using-ai-tools-with-their-people-ops-teams)) (30 June)
