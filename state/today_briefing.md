# Daily AI Recruitment Briefing — 11 July 2026

## Claude & Anthropic in TA
- Anthropic shipped **Claude Reflect**, a built-in dashboard that visualizes how you use Claude — top topics, most active day and peak hour, overall usage patterns — plus optional break reminders and quiet hours, in beta for Free, Pro, and Max users with memory on. For teams piloting Claude in sourcing or coordination work, it's a lightweight way to see where AI is actually being leaned on day-to-day — useful adoption and over-reliance signal to watch before you formalize governance. ([Anthropic's new Claude feature is quietly selling you on AI — TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/09/anthropics-new-claude-feature-is-quietly-selling-you-on-ai/)) (9 July)

## AI in Recruitment — What's New
- **Recruiter impersonation is now an AI-scaled threat to your employer brand:** fraudsters posing as staffing firms (including a fake version of Doherty's recruiting team) are blasting out AI-written job ads tailored to individuals' real job interests to harvest confidential candidate data, as generative tools cut the cost of a convincing fake to minutes. The FBI logged roughly 22,000 AI-enabled scam complaints last year; TA leaders should publish and promote official application channels, warn candidates proactively, and monitor for brand impersonation. ([Employment seekers and businesses navigate rise in AI-fueled fake job postings — The Spokesman-Review](https://www.spokesman.com/stories/2026/jul/07/employment-seekers-and-businesses-navigate-rise-in/)) (7 July)

**Bottom line:** As AI both accelerates your recruiting stack and arms the scammers targeting your candidates, the same instinct covers both — build visibility now, into how your team uses AI and into how your brand is being impersonated, before either gets ahead of your controls.
