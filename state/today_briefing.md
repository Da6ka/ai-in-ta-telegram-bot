# Daily AI Recruitment Briefing — 19 July 2026

## Claude & Anthropic in TA
- LTM struck a partnership with Anthropic to embed Claude, Claude Code, and Claude Cowork across enterprise delivery, committing to a dedicated Center of Excellence and the training of thousands of certified AI specialists and forward-deployed engineers. For TA leaders, it's another marquee services firm building its AI-implementation bench through certification pipelines rather than lateral poaching — reinforcing "AI specialist / FDE" as a distinct, fast-hardening hiring category to define and source for now. ([MarTech Series](https://martechseries.com/predictive-ai/ai-platforms-machine-learning/ltm-partners-with-anthropic-to-accelerate-claude-adoption-and-expand-enterprise-delivery/)) (13 July 2026)

## AI in Recruitment — What's New
- Xobin unveiled NURA, an agentic AI built for enterprise hiring that parses up to a million résumés in ~10 minutes, runs structured AI interviews, evaluates candidates, and schedules qualified talent with human recruiters — already in use at four of the top ten Indian IT services firms and two of the top five Indian banks. Before letting a single agent screen-to-schedule at that volume, pressure-test its evaluation logic for auditability and adverse-impact monitoring. ([PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/in/news-releases/xobin-unveils-nura-an-ai-agent-built-for-enterprise-hiring-302824553.html)) (14 July 2026)

**Bottom line:** The steady drumbeat of end-to-end agentic hiring launches — and enterprises staffing up to deploy AI at scale — means a TA leader's next moves are twofold: build sourcing pipelines for the AI-specialist/FDE roles now in demand, and demand documented human review and adverse-impact testing from any agent you let touch candidates.
